DDR2

DDR2 memory was introduced in 2003, its primary benefit over DDR being the ability to operate at twice the bus speed.
DDR2 DIMMs have a maximum capacity of 4 Gibibytes.
DDR3 was released in 2007, so DDR2 is completely obsolete.
