Chipsets

The Northbridge chipset on a motherboard, unlike the Southbridge chipset, is a microchip that is directly connected to the CPUHello . It's responsible for communications among the CPU and occasionally the RAM and PCI-E slots. Depending on the system, the motherboard may have a Northbridge and a Southbridge, or it may have only a Northbridge, and over time, various functions of the Northbridge have been reassigned to the CPU itself.
