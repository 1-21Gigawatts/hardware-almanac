Intel Chipsets

2016

Union Point

Compatible with "Skylake" processors on Socket LGA 1151.
Not much is currently known, although it is expected to support the coming PCIe 4.0 standard.

2015

Sunrise Point

Compatible with "Skylake" processors on Socket LGA 1151.
Includes chipsets H110, H170, Z170, B150, Q150, and Q170.
Anticipated to be relased in August of 2015.

2014

Wellsburg

Compatible with processors on Socket LGA 2011v3.
It is chipset X99.
It was released in August of 2014.


Wildcat Point

Compatible with "Haswell", "Haswell Refresh", and "Broadwell" processors on Socket LGA 1150.
Includes chipsets Z97 and H97.
It was released in May of 2014.

2013

Lynx Point

Compatible with "Haswell" and "Haswell Refresh" processors on Socket LGA 1150.
Includes chipsets H81, Q85, B85, Q87, H87, and Z87.
It was released in June of 2013.
Note that "Lynx Point" motherboards may require a BIOS update before they can be used with "Haswell Refresh" processors. Most 8-series motherboards, however, should support them out of the box.

2012

Panther Point

Compatible with "Sandy Bridge" and "Ivy Bridge" processors on Socket LGA 1155.
Includes chipsets Z77, Z75, H77, Q77, Q75, and B75.
It was released in April and May of 2012.

2011

Patsburg

Compatible with processors on Socket LGA 2011.
It is chipset X79.
It was released in November of 2011.


Cougar Point

Compatible with "Sandy Bridge" and "Ivy Bridge" processors on Socket LGA 1155.
Includes chipsets H61, P67, H67, Z68, Q67, Q65, and B65.
It was released in early 2011.

2009

Ibex Peak

Compatible with "Lynnfield" and "Clarkdale" processors on Socket LGA 1156.
Includes chipsets P55, H55, H57, and Q57.
It debuted in September of 2009, but the last three chipsets launched in early 2010.

2008

Tylersburg

Compatible with processors on Socket LGA 1366.
It is chipset X58.
It was released in November of 2008.
