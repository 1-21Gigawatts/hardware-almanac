Sockets

CPUs connect to the motherboard via their sockets.

Typically, Intel's sockets have pins that touch their CPUs.

Intel Sockets

2015

LGA 1151

Compatible with "Skylake" processors. Compatible with chipsets: H110, H170, Z170, B150, Q150, and Q170. Anticipated to be relased in August of 2015.
2014

LGA 2011v3

Compatible with "Haswell-E", "Haswell-EP", "Haswell-EX". Compatible with chipset: X99.
2013

LGA 1150 (H3)

Compatible with "Haswell", "Haswell Refresh", "Haswell-WS", and "Broadwell" processors. Compatible with chipsets: H81, B85, Q85, Q87, H87, Z87, H97, and Z97.
